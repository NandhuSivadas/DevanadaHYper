About HyperBoost

HyperBoost is an AI-driven hypertension risk assessment platform built to support early prediction and preventive care. Our system analyzes clinical, demographic, and lifestyle attributes to deliver accurate and accessible insights.

Designed with a strong focus on health awareness, HyperBoost empowers individuals to make proactive health choices using evidence-based analytics and modern machine learning techniques.
